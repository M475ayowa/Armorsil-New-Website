Before You Begin

Exam
Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before starting your Onboarding Final Examination. Violating any rule may result in automatic disqualification.

Rules & Guidelines

What You Need to Know

Follow every rule to ensure your exam is valid.

Exam Format: The exam has two sections. Section A contains 30 objective questions (2 marks each). Section B contains 5 theory questions — answer any 3 (10 marks each). Total: 90 marks. You need 70% (63 marks) to pass.

Time Limit: You have 45 minutes to complete the exam. The timer starts as soon as you enter the exam page and cannot be paused. Your answers will be submitted automatically when time runs out.

Malpractice Warning: Any form of malpractice — including but not limited to copying, impersonation, use of unauthorized materials, or communication with others during the exam — will result in automatic disqualification and a score of zero.

Tab Switch Policy: If you leave this tab or open another tab during the exam, the system will detect it. Your exam will be cancelled immediately and your score will be recorded as zero. Do not navigate away from the exam page for any reason.

Proctoring Notice: For the integrity of this assessment, camera access may be requested. This is a standard proctoring measure. Your camera feed is not recorded or stored — it is used only for live monitoring during the exam session.

Submission: Once you submit your exam (or when time expires), your objective section will be auto-graded. Your theory answers will be flagged for manual review. The result modal will show your score and pass/fail status immediately.

Ready to Begin?

Make sure you are in a quiet environment with a stable internet connection. Once you click the button below, you will be asked to grant camera access before proceeding to the exam.
