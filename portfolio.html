Our Work

Projects That Speak for Themselves

From high-rise towers and bridges to tunnels and industrial facilities — we deliver construction chemical solutions and engineering excellence across Africa.

Beyond Products

Engineering Solutions, Not Just Chemicals

At Armorsil West Africa, we don't just supply construction chemicals — we apply them. Our team of experienced engineers works hands-on with every client, from concept to completion. Whether it's waterproofing a high-rise foundation, grouting a bridge anchorage, or restoring a concrete tunnel lining, we bring the expertise and the products together to deliver lasting results. Every project in our portfolio represents a partnership built on trust, technical rigour, and a shared commitment to quality.

What We Do

Full-Spectrum Construction & Chemical Application

Building Construction

Waterproofing, concrete admixtures, flooring systems, and protective coatings for commercial, residential, and industrial buildings.

Bridges & Highways

Structural grouting, expansion joint sealants, concrete repair, and waterproof coatings for bridge decks, piers, and road infrastructure.

Tunnels & Underground

Crystalline waterproofing, shotcrete admixtures, and injection grouting for tunnels, culverts, and below-grade structures.

Water Management

Waterproofing of waterlogged areas, reservoirs, water treatment plants, and drainage systems using advanced chemical solutions.

Industrial Facilities

Epoxy flooring, chemical-resistant coatings, thermal insulation, and structural repair for factories, warehouses, and processing plants.

Concrete Restoration

Repair mortars, bonding agents, cathodic protection, and structural strengthening for ageing concrete structures.
