Who We Are

A Legacy of Innovation in Construction Chemicals

ARMORSIL West Africa is a subsidiary of ARMORSIL USA, a multinational manufacturer of specialty construction chemicals with a presence in the United States, United Kingdom, Italy, Turkey, and several countries across Europe and Africa. Established in 2012 and headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, we have grown to become a trusted partner in the construction industry across West and East Africa.

At Armorsil, we sell solutions — not just products. We take the time to understand each client's unique needs and challenges, conduct in-depth research in our well-equipped laboratories, and develop tailored solutions that deliver measurable results. Whether it's a high-rise tower in Lagos, a bridge across the Niger, or an industrial facility in Accra, our products are engineered to perform under the toughest conditions.

Having alliances with global manufacturers across Europe and North America, including Armorsil, allows us to provide the most suitable product solutions without being restricted to one particular system or product range — regardless of our client's location in Africa.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Construction Chemical Solutions

Waterproofing Admixtures

Crystalline and water-repellent admixtures for integral concrete waterproofing.

Concrete Admixtures

Super plasticizers, accelerators, and retarders for optimized concrete performance.

Waterproof Coating & Membrane

Cementitious, acrylic, polyurethane, and bitumen coatings for surface waterproofing.

Flooring Systems

Epoxy flooring, floor hardeners, and self-leveling compounds for industrial and commercial floors.

Tiling Solutions

Tile adhesives, grouts, and joint fillers for walls, floors, and external applications.

Structural Grout & Repair

Non-shrink grouts, repair mortars, bonding agents, and plugging compounds for concrete restoration.
